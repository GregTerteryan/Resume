High School

Crescenta Valley, Glendale Unified School District, CA

Normal Classes:
Intro to Engineering

-A class that introduces many different types of engineering, such an computer, electrical, and more. In computer engineering, we disassebled and re-assembled a laptop (with instructions). In electrical engineering, we used an Arduino to turn on LEDs in certain patterns. In the 2nd semester, I made a car that runs off Arduino and is controlled by an infrared remote.
Grade: A 1st semester


Honors/AP Classes:
AP Computer Science A

-A class where you learn how to program in Java and delve further into it. The class covers methods, classes, quadratic sort, and mergesort.
Grade: A both semesters.
AP Exam Score: 5


AP Human Geography

-A class where you learn about the recent history of the world, and learn geography in addition to the cultures of different places around the world. As my teacher would say, "The Why of Where"
Grade: A both semesters


Int 3B/Precalculus Accelerated (Honors Precalculus)

-A class that teaches the last semester of Integrated 3 and all of Precalculus in one year. Heavily focused on trigonometry, and introduces radians, angle and length speeds, the laws of sine and cosine, and more.
Grade: A 1st semester


Honors Software Development Internship

-A class where student are given a semester to make a programming project. Projects can be anything that the teacher approves, ranging from games to websites to mobile apps and more. My project was an RPG (Role-Play-Game) in which the player can click on a menu to battle enemies and eventually beat the final boss in the final area. Further explained in the "Projects" tab.
